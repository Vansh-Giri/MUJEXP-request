Account and Data Deletion for MUJ Experience Theatre

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data from the MUJ Experience Theatre application.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

Upon receiving and verifying your request, we will permanently delete the following information associated with your account:

  • Your user account, including your email address from our authentication system.
  • Your name and any other profile information.
  • Your history of all booked tickets.

How to Request Deletion

To request the deletion of your account, please send an email to our support team by clicking the link below.

Important: Please send the email from the same email address that you used to register your account. This is required for us to verify your identity.

Click here to send an account deletion request

Alternatively, if the link above does not work, please manually send an email to: mujexperienceapp@gmail.com

We will process your request within 7-14 business days.
